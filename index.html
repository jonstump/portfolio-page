Why am I taking an Intro to Programming Class?

I am taking an intro to programming class for a few reasons:

  1. I would like to get into leadership. With the current need for programmers being around 2 Million just within the United States that means we will need more leaders to help organize and co-ordinate these new programmers.
  2. With every passing day programming is becoming a core skill that many people will need. I envision a day where many of us will have personal servers for our email and other tools for better privacy and control over our data.
  3. I always enjoy learning new things. While I have previously learned some coding with SQL and Python it was in an unstructured format. I wanted something that would keep me honest and push me forward with my learning.
